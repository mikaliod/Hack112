Basic Instructions

Map You can view the map of the campus and check out all the locations of the portals in the Map Page. Hack button is at the bottom-right corner.
Hack When you are near a portal, by pressing the Hack button, you can hack the portal. More rules of hacking will be explained later on this page.
Portals You can check out the status of the portals in the portals status page, whether they're occupied or not, or the owner team of the portal.

Hack Rules

Unoccupied Portals When a portal is unoccupied, either team can hack the portal and take over the portal.
Attack Portals When a portal is occupied by a team, the other team can attack the portal. A occupied portal needs two attacks to be taken over by another team.
Shield State When a portal has been attacked, it will immediately go into a shield state of one hour. During this period, nobody can attack the tower.
Heal When a portal has been attacked, its owner team can use the hack button to heal the portal. Healing the tower resulting in a recover from the damage of one attack.
